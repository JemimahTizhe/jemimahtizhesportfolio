Hello Again, I'm Jemimah Tizhe

I am a creative and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer, Social Media Manager, Graphics Designer, and Frontend Developer. I specialize in transforming ideas into intuitive, visually appealing experiences across web and digital platforms.

Over the years, I've worked with clients from different industries to design brand identities, craft user-centered websites, manage digital communities, and launch online stores and food businesses like Mima's Online Store and Mima's Fast Food.

I am passionate about making designs that are functional and user-friendly. My goal is to create digital products that are not only beautiful but also meaningful.

When I'm not working on a design or writing code, you'll find me exploring new tools, building my personal brand, or helping others bring their ideas to life.

A passionate techie with a good foundation in computer science. With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Igbinedion University, Okada, Edo State.

I gained experience at organizations such as Ha-Shem Limited and New Horizons, I specialize in Ui/Ux Designs, Frontend Development, and Graphics Designs to drive informed decision-making and enhance creative performance. My journey in this tech field has been marked by impactful personal projects.

Beyond my endeavors, I've also contributed to remote projects with my friends in my field to up my tech game. As I continue to explore Frontend Development, Social Media Management, UI/UX, and Graphic Design, I remain driven by excellence and a passion for leveraging tech-driven strategies to achieve success.

Join me on this journey as I showcase my portfolio and share insights into my tech-driven approach to problem-solving. Let's connect and explore opportunities to collaborate on transformative projects together.I believe that the success of a project is based on good collaboration.
