Asta Beauty Corner

Client: Hyelni Stanley Balami

Project Type: Freelance Design Work

Tools Used: Canva / Adobe Photoshop

Duration: October 2024 - Till Date

Role: Graphic Designer

Project Overview

I worked with Asta Beauty Corner, a luxury unisex beauty salon in Ikeja, Lagos, to design creative and visually appealing graphics that reflect their brand identity.

My Role:

  • Designing promotional flyers and social media posts to showcase their services (braids, pedicures, frontals, and more).
  • Creating a consistent visual style aligned with their luxury brand image.
  • Enhancing customer engagement through attractive, professional, and brand-focused designs for Instagram.
