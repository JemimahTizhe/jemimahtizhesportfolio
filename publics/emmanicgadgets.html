Emmanic Gadgets

Project: Shipping Service Promotional Flyer

Client: Emmanic Gadget Store CEO

Role: Graphic Designer

Tools Used: Canva

Overview

Emmanic Gadget Store approached me to create an engaging and professional flyer to promote their international gadget shipping service to customers in Nigeria. The goal was to clearly communicate the types of gadgets they accept, the shipping process, and the terms and conditions, while maintaining a visually appealing layout that reflects their brand identity.

Design Approach

  • Color Scheme: Incorporated the brand's colors—deep navy blue, white, and a touch of red—to convey trust, professionalism, and urgency.
  • Typography: Used bold and clean sans-serif fonts for headings to grab attention, and smaller, readable fonts for detailed information to ensure clarity.
  • Layout: Balanced text and visuals with a left-right structure—important information on the left and an impactful logistics image on the right to reinforce the service theme.

Visual Elements

  • Included a large "SHIP" headline for instant recognition.
  • Used bullet points to make the “Ship gadgets like” section easily scannable.
  • Integrated brand logo prominently for recognition.
  • Added a high-quality transport and shipping imagery to visually connect with the service.

Call to Action

Clearly displayed contact information, social media handle, and physical address at the bottom for quick accessibility.

Outcome

The flyer effectively captured attention both online and offline. The strategic use of color, typography, and visual hierarchy ensured the message was clear, professional, and aligned with the company's branding. This piece helped boost inquiries from potential customers looking to ship gadgets abroad.
