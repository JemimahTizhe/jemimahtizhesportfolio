Zamami Fabrics - Promotional Flyer Design

Client: Ijudigal Yakubu

Project Type: Freelance Design Work

Tools Used: Canva / Adobe Photoshop

Duration: September 2025

Role: Graphic Designer

Project Overview

This flyer was designed for Zamami Fabrics, a brand specializing in high-quality women’s fabrics. The goal was to create an engaging promotional piece that highlights new additions to their collection while maintaining a stylish and premium feel.

Design Approach

The layout combines vibrant fabric photography with a warm, textured background that evokes richness and elegance. Key details such as the business name, product offering, and contact information were arranged strategically for clarity and flow.

  • Typography: A clean, modern font with emphasis on “WOMENS FABRICS” to immediately capture attention.
  • Color Scheme: Deep, earthy tones paired with golden highlights to reflect the luxury and authenticity of African fabrics.
  • Imagery: Close-up shots of the fabrics were featured in a grid-style arrangement to showcase texture, detail, and variety.
  • Brand Elements: Social media handles and contact details were integrated seamlessly to encourage customer engagement.

Results

The final flyer communicates professionalism, vibrancy, and cultural authenticity. It effectively positions Zamami Fabrics as a go-to brand for elegant women’s fabrics while ensuring the design is versatile for both print and digital promotion.
