Send Us Logistics - Logo Design

Brand: Send Us Logistics.

Date: September 2025.

Tools Used: Canva

Project Overview

Send Us Logistics is a modern dispatch and delivery company focused on speed, reliability, and professionalism. I was commissioned to create a brand logo that reflects these values while standing out in the logistics industry.

Visual Decoration

  • Style: A 3D emblem design that conveys strength and modernity.
  • Typography: Clean, corporate typefaces to reinforce professionalism and clarity.
  • Color Palette: Black, White and gold to represent luxury, trust, and excellence, with flexible variations in silver and white for different brand materials.
  • Style: Clean layout that works well on product tags, social media, and packaging
  • Application: Adaptable across helmets, delivery boxes, bikes, uniforms, and digital platforms.

Results

The final logo gives Send Us Logistics a sleek, corporate identity that communicates trust and reliability. It is scalable, versatile, and impactful — making it a strong foundation for the company’s overall branding system.
