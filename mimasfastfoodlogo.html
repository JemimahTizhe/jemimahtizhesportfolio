About Mima's Fast Food Logo

Project Overview:

Mima's Fast Food is a vibrant and modern eatery offering delicious, quick, and affordable meals. The brand was in need of a fresh and appetizing visual identity that could stand out in the fast food industry and appeal to a youthful, food-loving audience.

Design Goals:

  • Create a fun, visually inviting logo that evokes hunger and warmth.
  • Use playful yet clean typography to balance professionalism with approachability.
  • Develop a flexible identity that works across menus, packaging, signage, and digital platforms.
  • Incorporate food-themed elements or colors (like red, yellow, orange) to stimulate appetite and energy.

Concept & Visual Style:

A friendly, bold font paired with a recognizable icon (e.g., a burger, chef hat, or flame) was used to create instant recall.

Color Palette:

A mix of warm tones (reds and yellows) was chosen for their psychological effect — they increase appetite and convey joy, energy, and urgency.

Deliverables:

  • Logo (primary and simplified versions)
  • Food packaging design (e.g., burger wraps, fries containers, takeaway bags)
  • Social media templates (promotions, menu highlights, combo offers)
  • Storefront signage concept
  • Menu board design

Tools Used:

  • Canva / Figma (Social Media Layouts)

Client Feedback:

The client loved the vibrant personality of the design and how well it visually represented the brand’s mission to serve happiness through food. The new look helped increase customer engagement and brand recognition both online and offline.
