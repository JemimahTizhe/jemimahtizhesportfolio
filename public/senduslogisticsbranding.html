Send Us Logistics (Branding Designs)

Client: Ayoola Soetan

Project Type: Freelance Design Work

Tools Used: Canva / Adobe Photoshop

Duration: September 2025

Role: Graphic Designer, Social Media Manager

Project Overview

Send Us Logistics is a fast-growing delivery and dispatch company that focuses on speed, reliability, and trust. I was tasked with creating a bold, professional brand identity that reflects efficiency while standing out in a competitive logistics market.

Design Approach

The branding combines modern minimalism with a corporate, sleek aesthetic. The logo is designed as a strong emblem that merges elements of a bicycle and a dispatch bike, symbolizing both agility and power. The use of black and gold represents luxury, professionalism, and excellence, while alternate color variations (white and black backgrounds) ensure adaptability across multiple platforms.

Execution

  • Logo: A 3D emblem design with metallic gold and silver finishes, creating a premium, high-impact look.
  • Typography: Clean, bold typefaces to emphasize clarity and corporate strength.
  • Color Palette: Black, white, and gold – balancing elegance with trustworthiness.
  • Mockups: Application across bikes, helmets, delivery boxes, and marketing materials to demonstrate real-world usage.

Results

The final identity positions Send Us Logistics as a dependable, forward-thinking brand. The branding system is versatile, scalable, and memorable – ensuring the company’s image resonates strongly both online and offline.
