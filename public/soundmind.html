Soundmind Gadgets

Client: Soundmind Gadgets

Project Type: Freelance Design Work

Duration: September 2023 - Till Date

Tools Used: Canva / Adobe Photoshop / Figma

Role: Graphic Designer and Social Media Manager

Project Overview

I worked with Auspis Prime to create a series of eye-catching, on-brand flyers designed to promote their services and engage their target audience. The goal was to deliver visually appealing designs that communicate their message clearly and professionally across both digital and print formats.

Design Concept

  • Clean, professional layout
  • Brand-aligned color palette and typography
  • Balanced use of text and visuals for easy readability

Outcome

The flyers helped present the company's offerings in a polished and engaging way, contributing to a stronger visual presence during their promotions.

What I Learned:

This project taught me how to take a brand vision and translate it into a clear visual identity using Canva. I learned how font pairing, spacing, and color selection can create a logo that speaks directly to the brand's audience — women who love stylish, affordable fashion.

My Role:

I designed the Mima's Online Store logo from scratch using Canva — carefully selecting fonts, refining layout, and customizing elements to match the brand's personality and target market.
