Practice Logo Design - LS Monogram

Project Type: Personal Practice

Design Inspired By: YouTube Tutorial

Date: Feburary 2024

Overview

The LS Logo was one of my creative explorations in logo design, inspired by techniques I learned through YouTube tutorials. This project allowed me to practice combining typography with modern design principles to create a clean and professional brand mark.

What I Learned

  • How to combine letters creatively to form a cohesive monogram
  • Use of symmetry and spacing to maintain visual balance
  • How to apply mockup rendering for a realistic 3D look
  • Fundamentals of using gold and black color contrast to express luxury and professionalism

Design Features:

  • Intertwined “L” and “S” letters in a strong serif font
  • Circular frame with text alignment to simulate a seal or premium badge
  • Both black & white and gold 3D versions created to test versatility across brand styles

Tools Used:

  • Adobe Illustrator (Vector Design)
  • Adobe Photoshop (Mockup & 3D Presentation)

Purpose:

Though this was not for a real client, the project served as a hands-on opportunity to practice layout composition, monogram styling, and branding presentation, contributing to my design growth and confidence.
