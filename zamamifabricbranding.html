Zamami Fabrics - Branded Packaging Design

Client: Ijudigal Yakubu

Project Type: Freelance Design Work

Tools Used: Canva / Adobe Photoshop

Duration: September 2025

Role: Graphic Designer

Project Overview

This project involved designing a custom shopping bag for Zamami Fabrics, a fashion brand that specializes in premium fabrics. The aim was to create packaging that not only carries products but also communicates the brand’s elegance and identity.

Design Concept

The design features the Zamami Fabrics logo prominently in the center, paired with the brand’s tagline: “Your fabric, Your style, Your story.” This reinforces the brand’s core message of individuality and cultural expression.

Visual Direction

  • Typography: Elegant serif fonts with ornamental flourishes, reflecting sophistication and timeless style.
  • Color Palette: A deep brown accent on a clean white background, creating a contrast that feels classy and professional.
  • Layout: Social media icons and contact details are clearly displayed at the bottom, ensuring accessibility and brand visibility.
  • Functionality: Designed for practical use while doubling as a subtle form of mobile advertising, as customers carry the bag in public.

Results

The final design enhances the customer experience, turning every purchase into an opportunity for brand promotion. It communicates elegance, professionalism, and accessibility, aligning perfectly with Zamami Fabrics' identity.
